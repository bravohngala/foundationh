Who we are

Who We Are For over 6 years Super Eagles Travel and Tours Ltd has been providing an unrivalled full spectrum of business and leisure travel services that our satisfied customers will certainly attest to. We pride ourselves in offering world class services in the regions we operate. Our well experienced consultants offer clients with most economical, practical and convenient travel solutions. We have received numerous awards and recognitions since inception. We have established a reputation in the industry for having strong and long lasting relationships with both our local and international clients.

Our vision is To become the most preferred travel agent in providing services to a wide clientele base ranging from private sector, Government agencies , Non govermental and individuals.
